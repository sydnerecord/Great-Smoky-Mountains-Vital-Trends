5.Model_radius_ReadMe

Import & clean: Loads fish (three-pass), macroinvertebrate (genus/EPT/NCBI), soils, and forest health (trees + seedlings) datasets; standardizes site IDs, joins coordinates, and derives site-year summaries (richness, densities/biomass, seedling stems).


Climate stacks: Loads PRISM annual mean precipitation and temperature rasters for GRSM for later extraction.


Coverage analysis: Converts taxa to sf, projects to UTM 17N, computes nearest cross-taxon distances, and evaluates % coverage vs search radii (800–5000 m) from fish and invert baselines.


Clustering (3.2 km): Builds connected-component clusters of all taxa points using a 3,200 m threshold; creates cluster centroids.


Cluster-year assembly: Maps site records to cluster IDs and aggregates to cluster × year (means for continuous metrics; sums for abundances), producing fish, invert, plant, and soil panels joined into a single cluster_year table.


Attach climate: Extracts PRISM precipitation (mm) and temperature (°C) at cluster centroids by year and merges into cluster_year.


Diagnostics: Reports counts of total points, clusters, and missing cluster assignments.


Visualization helpers:


plot_yearly_trend() → median ± IQR by year across clusters.


plot_spaghetti() → cluster trajectories (optionally subsampled) with a bold median overlay; log-y scaling.


plot_z_heatmap() → z-score heatmaps by variable with clusters ordered by a reference year.


Correlation matrix: Computes Spearman ρ among fish, invert, plant, soil, and climate variables at the cluster-year level and plots a heatmap.


Side effect: Writes an enriched fish summary with locations to CSV for reuse.
